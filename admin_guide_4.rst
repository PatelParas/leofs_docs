| Command                                              | Explanation                                                                       |

+------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

+------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| remove `{GATEWAY_NODE}`                              | * Retrieve status of the cluster                                                  |

+------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| dump-ring `{MANAGER}`|`{STORAGE}`|`{GATEWAY}`        | Dump ring and memeber-info [1.0.0-pre1 or higher ]                                |

| history                                              | Retrieve history of operations                                                    |
+------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
